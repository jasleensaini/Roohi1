Roohi

Like grapes, we have always
    accompanied the vat.
From the view of the world,
    we have disappeared.

For years, we boiled from
   the fire of love
Until we became that wine
    which intoxicates the world.

Sometimes it's better

to hide

in order to

survive evil

Thanatos

This fall

Prepare

Refresh to replay
